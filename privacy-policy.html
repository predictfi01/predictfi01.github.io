Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2025

1. General

1.1 This Policy explains how PredictFi collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information generated through your use of the platform.

1.2 By using this platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information Basic information provided at registration, including email address and username.

2.2 Transaction Information Deposit and withdrawal records, trading history, settlement outcomes, and account balance changes.

2.3 Wallet Address External wallet addresses linked to your account (where applicable), used solely for processing deposits and withdrawals. PredictFi does not store private keys or seed phrases.

2.4 Device & Log Information IP address, browser type, access timestamps, and behavioral logs, used for security protection and issue diagnosis.

2.5 Compliance Information Identity verification materials may be required when compliance reviews are triggered, such as for large withdrawals.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve platform services
  • Process deposits, withdrawals, and trade settlements
  • Conduct identity verification and compliance screening (KYC / AML)
  • Detect and prevent fraud, money laundering, and other prohibited conduct
  • Send service notifications and security alerts
  • Fulfill legal obligations and respond to regulatory requirements

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Exceptions include:

  • Service Providers: Necessary information may be shared with technical, payment, and compliance service providers who assist in platform operations. These parties are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: Disclosure may occur as required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, relevant information may be transferred accordingly. You will be notified in advance.

5. Data Storage & Retention

5.1 Your data is stored on PredictFi's servers and may be subject to cross-border transfer. We ensure that applicable data protection requirements are met.

5.2 Following account closure, we retain necessary data (e.g., transaction records for compliance purposes) for the minimum period required by applicable law. All other data will be deleted or anonymized.

6. Security Measures

We employ encryption in transit, access controls, and audit logging to protect your data. However, no internet transmission can be guaranteed to be entirely secure, and we cannot warrant absolute data security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and correct your account information
  • Request a portable export of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data to the extent permitted by law
  • Withdraw consent for specific data processing activities (without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing)

To exercise any of the above rights, please contact us using the details at the end of this Policy.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze platform usage. You may manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, though certain features may be affected as a result.

9. Minors

This platform is not intended for users under the age of 18. If we become aware that data has been inadvertently collected from a minor, it will be promptly deleted.

Material changes to this Policy will be announced prominently on the platform in advance. Continued use of the platform following such notice constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
